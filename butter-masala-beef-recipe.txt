Ingredients:
 - 1 12.5-ounce jar Maya Kaimal Butter Masala Indian Simmer Sauce
 - 1 pound ground beef
 - 1 tablespoon olive oil
 - 1 yellow squash
 - 1 medium yellow onion
 - 1 teaspoon Tony Chachere's Original Creole Seasoning
 - 2 teaspoon Stonemill Essentials Italian Seasoning
 - cooked pasta or rice
 
 Directions:
 1. Chop onion and squash into small 1/2 inch pieces
 2. Heat oil in a frying/saute pan till it shimmers (medium high heat)
 3. Add onion and squash and saute for 2 minutes
 4. Add the ground beef and brown for 4 minutes
 5. Sprinkle the Creole Seasoning over the ground beef
 6. Add simmer sauce and mix together
 7. Sprinkle on the Italian Seasoning
 8. Simmer on low heat for 15 minutes
 9. Spoon over cooked pasta or rice
